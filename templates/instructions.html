Getting Started

This app allows you to set a timer for your meditation sessions and select music from Freesound that matches your preference during the duration of your session.

Steps to Use the App

  • Set your Meditation Duration: Enter the number of minutes you want to meditate in the input box labeled "Meditation Duration".
  • Search for Sounds: Type a keyword related to the type of meditation music you want, such as "relaxation", "nature sounds", or "ambient".
  • Select a Sound: Once you search, the app will show you a list of music options that match your selected duration. Select one from the dropdown list.
  • Start the Timer: Press the "Start Timer" button to begin your meditation session. The music will start playing, and the countdown will begin.
  • End of Meditation: When the timer reaches zero, the music will stop, and your session will be complete.

Tips for a Better Meditation Experience

  • Find a quiet, comfortable space where you can relax during your meditation session.
  • Close your eyes and focus on your breathing.
  • Try different types of sounds (nature sounds, music, etc.) to find what works best for you.
  • Start with shorter sessions and gradually increase the duration as you become more comfortable.
